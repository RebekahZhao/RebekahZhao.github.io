Research

I am interested in developing energy-efficient integrated circuits for wireless communication and Biomedical Implants. My research projects include:

  • Imaging with 60GHz microwave

    The goal of this project is to use s-parameter measurements of 60GHz millimeter waves reflected by objects to characterize high-resolution surface feature. An antenna is placed on both the transmission and receiving side. A motion stage moves the transmitter antenna on a 2D coordinate to scan target object with certain resolution. The transmitter sends waves of multiple frequencies, and phase information of S11 and S21 of each frequency is extracted and analyzed to achieve absolute distance of each scanning position. I am currently using the Agilent vector network analyzer to characterize the behavior of high-frequency millimeter waves. [Slides]
